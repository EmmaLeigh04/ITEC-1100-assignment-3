LitMas Holiday Fun

It’s the night before Christmas. Santa’s magical energy is too powerful and is corrupting the mainframe. All the Christmas lights are turning off and without them Santa won’t be able to find his way to the homes of all the children in town. Your job as Santa’s top Elf is to carefully sneak into the homes and keep all the lights on for Santa. Better get cracking, there’s a lot of ground to cover tonight!





Instructions


  1. Use Left/Right arrows to move the elf.

  2. Press Space to fix tree lights when you are close to the object.

  3. Fix all lights before Santa arrives (watch the shrinking timer bar!).

  4. If Santa arrives and lights are still broken → Game Over.
