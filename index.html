Elf Fixer — Night Before Christmas

Help the elf fix Christmas tree lights before Santa arrives!

Play House 1 Play House 2

How to Play

  1. Use Left/Right arrows to move the elf.
  2. Press Space to fix tree lights when you’re close to a tree.
  3. Fix all lights before Santa arrives (watch the shrinking timer bar!).
  4. If Santa arrives and lights are still broken → Game Over.
